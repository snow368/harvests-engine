Focus areas (product still images only):

9. Fine-Grained Visual Inference (NEW — infer these from visual evidence)
For each coarse category, output fine-grained parameters using visual reasoning rules:

LIGHTING FINE:
- light_direction: Infer from shadow direction relative to product
- light_hardness: Infer from shadow edge quality (crisp=hard, soft edge=diffused)
- light_count: Infer from highlight count and shadow complexity
- contrast_level: Infer from highlight-to-shadow luminance ratio
- temperature_feeling: Infer from color cast on neutral/white areas

POSITION FINE:
- product_angle: Infer from product surfaces visible (0° straight-on, 45° three-quarter, etc.)
- distance: Infer from product-to-frame fill ratio
- framing: Infer from margin space around product (tight/medium/loose)
- perspective: Infer from camera height relative to product (eye-level/high angle/low angle)

MATERIAL FINE:
- specular_intensity: Infer from highlight sharpness (pinpoint=highly specular, broad=diffuse)
- surface_roughness: Infer from light scatter pattern (sharp reflection=smooth, scattered=rough)
- material_category: Infer from combined visual properties (metal/plastic/rubber/paper)

CAMERA FINE:
- focal_length_equivalent: Infer from perspective and distortion
- aperture_feeling: Infer from depth of field width
- camera_movement_style: Infer from frame stability (locked down vs handheld)

COMPOSITION FINE:
- visual_weight_distribution: Infer from product position in frame
- depth_construction: Infer from number of visible depth planes
- negative_space_ratio: Infer from product-to-frame area ratio

BACKGROUND FINE:
- background_material: Infer from texture and reflectivity of background
- background_depth: Infer from shadow sharpness and focus falloff

NEEDLE & CARTRIDGE DETAIL (MANDATORY when productCategory=tattoo_cartridges or needle_pack):
When needle or cartridge detected, identify these visual features:

--- Needle Type (from tip arrangement, if macro/close-up) ---
RL = tight circle converged to one point, for lining
RS = loose circle with gaps between needles, for shading
RM = medium circle between RL and RS
M1 = two parallel flat rows (oval shape), for color packing
CM = curved/arched rows, smooth shading
F = single straight line row, precise detail
Bugpin = visibly thinner (<0.30mm), fine detail work
OpenLiner = parallel non-converging wide arrangement (Cheyenne)

--- Brand Identification (from housing color + label style) ---
black + gold ring = Kwadron (high confidence)
translucent blue = Cheyenne Safety / translucent pink = Cheyenne Craft / translucent purple = Cheyenne Capillary
clear/transparent shell = BigWasp / TATSoul / Stigma (check label for distinction)
metal / rainbow PVD = BlackClaw (high confidence)
rainbow anodized = Dragonhawk (high confidence)
solid black (no gold ring) = CNC
frosted/matte translucent = MagicMoon
white shell = FK Irons rebrand

--- Membrane Type (from cartridge base view) ---
white opaque rubber = white_standard (most common)
clear thin silicone = clear_thin (Kwadron signature)
white translucent silicone = silicone_high (Cheyenne Safety)

--- Connection Type (from cartridge base side profile) ---
screw_long/screw_short = standard universal compatibility
bayonet = twist-lock system (Ultima/MYM)
magnetic = magnetic bond (Scorpion/Cobra only)
push_fit = straight push-in (PMU machines)

--- Packaging Format ---
individual_blister = single sterile pack (premium)
multi_blister = multiple in one pack (value)
box_set = boxed bundle
loose_display = tray display (shop bulk)
bulk_tray = partition bulk tray (studio refill)

--- Flow Channel (流道, from transparent/translucent cartridge side view) ---
Requires transparent/translucent housing — metal/opaque housing blocks view
Wide gap = needle loose in tube, high flow rate (M1/CM filling) but may splatter
Tight gap = needle snug, controlled flow (RL lining) but may starve at speed
Moderate gap = balanced, versatile

Internal taper angle (from side profile of tip):
Steep = sharp funnel, ink concentration (fine control)
Shallow = gentle taper, wide dispersion (fill work)
Multi-stage = two+ angle zones, hybrid compound design (premium feature, Kwadron Hybrid)

--- productDetail string format ---
When needle/cartridge identified, assemble productDetail: {gauge}{count}{type}_{taper}
Example: 1003RL_LT (10 gauge, 3 count, Round Liner, Long Taper)
Use "unknown" for unidentifiable parts: xx03RL or xxxxM1
